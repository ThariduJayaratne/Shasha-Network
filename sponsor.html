Since 2014, our mission has been to level the playing field for talented young people from low aspirational backgrounds across the world. This has been made possible by individuals who believe that we can create a better future by making “little bets” on talented young people who hold the answers to some of tomorrow’s challenges.

Today, there is a young person in a village or small town, who is one step away from achieving their dream or unlocking the idea that brings electricity, health care or education to their community. However they lack the opportunity to access a world class education or stage to express their talent.Your contribution will help bridge that gap and access world class career development skills, a professional mentorship network and career opportunities.
