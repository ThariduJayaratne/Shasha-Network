Meet Our Tribe!

Our mission to close the career aspiration gap for young people across the world has attracted individuals from all walks of life and diverse professional backgrounds. At the core of what brings us together, is our shared passion for supporting young people to discover their purpose and reach their fullest potential. Since our inception, we have continued to inspire and attract seasoned professionals with backgrounds in education, industrial psychology, business strategy, academia, design thinking, technology and research.
